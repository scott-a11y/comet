## Executive Summary

**Comet** is a revolutionary shop layout and design platform that combines AI-powered floor plan analysis, real-time 3D collision detection, and intelligent system routing to help woodworking shops, cabinet manufacturers, and industrial facilities optimize their workspace before spending a single dollar on equipment or construction.

**Unique Value Proposition:** The only "shop-aware" design tool that understands machinery requirements, automatically detects equipment from photos, and generates material shopping listsâ€”eliminating costly layout mistakes and reducing shop setup time from weeks to hours.

**Target Market Size:** 
- 200,000+ woodworking shops in North America
- 50,000+ cabinet manufacturing facilities
- 100,000+ industrial makerspaces and educational facilities

**Revenue Model:** Tiered SaaS ($0-$299/mo) + Marketplace commissions

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Conversion from Free to Paid
**Mitigation:**
- Aggressive feature gating (AI analysis, BOM export behind paywall)
- Time-limited free trials of premium features
- "Upgrade to save this layout" prompts

### Risk 2: High Churn Due to One-Time Use
**Mitigation:**
- Multi-project workflows (design, then iterate, then execute)
- Ongoing value: equipment marketplace, system optimization
- Annual billing discount (15% off = 12-month commitment)

### Risk 3: Equipment Manufacturers Build Competing Tools
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage and brand establishment
- Partnerships and integrations (make them allies, not competitors)
- Multi-manufacturer neutrality (we're Switzerland)

### Risk 4: Market Education Required
**Mitigation:**
- Heavy content marketing (YouTube, blogs, case studies)
- Free tier to reduce barrier to trial
- Trade show demos and influencer partnerships
